Future Islands

About Future Islands

Future Islands is an American synthpop/alternative/indie band based in Balitmore, MD. The band is comprised of Samuel T. Herring, Gerrit Welmers, William Cashion, and Michael Lowry.

Members of the band pictured above. From left to right: Gerrit Welmers, William Cashion, Michael Lowry, and Samuel T. Herring

Band Members

  • Samuel T. Herring: lead vocals and lyrics
  • Gerrit Welmers: keyboardist
  • William Cashion: bassist
  • Michael Lowry: drums
